ABOUT

Eternal summer is our focus
& our state of mind

L’ETO was born from a philosophy that blends the
heart and mind of nutritious and healthy dining
complemented by staggering variety of desserts.
Every aspect of L’ETO was inspired by the core
components of summer: the bloom, freshness,
brightness, vividness and the enlightening sun.
These elements have been woven throughout the
menu as well as the design, architecture,
ambiance, light and sound.


LUXURY HOSPITALITY

Together at L'ETO

Started as a bright café in Soho, London, L’ETO has transformed into an all-day dining restaurant in over six countries including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Kuwait and Qatar.

Combining warm hospitality with intricate details and alluring aspects, L’ETO creates a journey of discovery in each and every visit, enticing the explorer within.


MENU CONCEPT

Cuisine & cakes beyond the
boundaries of taste

A local favorite, L’ETO has quickly become one of
the region’s most-visited cafes offering a
delicious cuisine and mouth-watering array of
Instagrammable breakfasts, lunch and dinners.
The upscale establishment, where the main star
is the food and the rich selection of salads &
mains, as well as coffee and desserts, the
luxurious and homely ambience makes L’ETO the
perfect fit for epicureans looking for an
exceptional dining experience to share.
